Qeema Aloo Recipe

Description

This is a hearty south Asian dish made with ground meat and potatos. The ground meat is usually beef, but could be lamb, goat, or chicken. This goes well with plain white rice or south Asian flat breads like naan, chapati/roti, or paratha.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef, 2 lbs.
  • Potatos, 4 medium size.
  • Onions, 2 medium size.
  • Tomatos, 3 medium size, or equivalent canned.
  • Ginger Paste, 2 tablespoons.
  • Garlic Paste, 3 tablespoons.
  • Salt, 2 teaspoons.
  • Red Chilli powder, 2 teaspoons.
  • Vegetable Oil, 2 tablespoons.
  • Fresh Cilantro, 1 bunch.
  • Frozen Peas 1/2 lbs (optional).

Steps

  1. Peel and finely chop the onions.
  2. Peel and cut the potatos into wedges about a quarter inch thick. Keep the cut potatos in water to prevent discoloration.
  3. Cut the tomatos into small pieces and keep for later.
  4. Pour the vegetable oil into a medium sized saucepan and place medium heat.
  5. Once the oil is hot add the chopped onions and saute untill golden brown.
  6. Once the onions are golden brown, add the ginger, garlic, salt, red chillies. Saute until ginger and garlic are golden brown.
  7. Add the tomatos and saute for 3-4 minutes.
  8. Add the ground meat and saute by stirring it around and breaking any clumps. Saute untill the meat is brown.
  9. Add the potatos, cover the pot and lower the heat. Let is simmer untill the potatos are done. You can check with a fork.
  10. Optionaly add the peas, cover and let simmer for another 10 minutes.
  11. Transfer the qeema aloo to a serving dish and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  12. Enjoy with boiled white rice, or with naan, roti/chipati, or paratha.
