Kachumar Salad Recipe

Description

Kachumar salad is a classic and is meant to be eaten with the main entries and not seperately. The main charactertics are that everthing is cut small and the dressing is tangy. There is a lot flexibility in the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • One medium sized tomato.
  • One medium sized cucumber.
  • One small red onion.
  • Two stalks of green onions.
  • A few small radishes.
  • One medium sized green hot pepper.
  • 1 medium sized lime or lemon.
  • Teaspoon of salt.
  • Tablespoon of white vinigar.
  • Cilantro leaves for garnish.

Steps

  1. Wash all the vegitables.
  2. Peal and chop the cucumber and red onion into small pieces.
  3. Chop the tomato, pepper, green onions, and radishes into small pieces.
  4. Place all the choped ingredients into a serving dish.
  5. Half or quarter the lemon/lime and squeeze the juice into the dish.
  6. Add the salt and vinigar.
  7. Mix everything together.
  8. Garnish with the cilantro leaves.
  9. Serve with the rest of the food.
